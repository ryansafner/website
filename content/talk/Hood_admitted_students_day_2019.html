Bitcoin

  • A radically decentralized peer-to-peer cryptographic currency
  • Created in 2009 by “Satoshi Nakamoto”
  • Open source: software is free and anyone can change it
  • Most recognizable cryptocurrency and application of blockchain technology

The Argument for Bitcoin

  • Free, instantaneous, usable by anyone (in theory)
  • A fixed supply of money
  • Tamper-proof
  • Requires no trust in any central authority
